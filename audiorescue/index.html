Features

💧
Quick Clean

15, 30, or 60-second sessions at 165Hz — the ideal frequency for clearing everyday moisture and splashes.

🌊
Deep Clean

A 30-second frequency sweep from 165Hz to 300Hz for more thorough moisture clearing after bigger spills.

⚡
Turbo Mode

High-intensity 400Hz bursts for stubborn water after full submersion or prolonged exposure.

🎛️
Custom Frequency

Manually dial in any frequency from 150Hz to 450Hz for precise, targeted results on any device.

🔔
Cleaner Reminders

Schedule weekly or monthly reminders to run a maintenance clean on your speakers.

🔒
Fully Private

No account required. Everything runs locally on your device — no data ever leaves.
