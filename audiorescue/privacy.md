# Privacy Policy for Audio Rescue
**Last updated:** June 3, 2026

## Introduction
Audio Rescue helps you run speaker-care sessions after water exposure. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app handles, how it is used, and the choices you have.

## Information We Store
Audio Rescue is designed to work primarily on your device. We do not require an account, and we do not ask for your name, email address, or phone number to use the core features.

The app may store settings locally on your device, including dark mode, haptic feedback, reminder preferences, advanced frequency selection, and last clean timestamp.

## Audio And Device Use
The app provides a native visual cleaning countdown, device-positioning guide, and local tone playback.

The app does not request microphone access, does not record your environment, and does not upload audio.

## Notifications
If you enable cleaner reminders, the app may request notification permission and schedule local reminders. Reminder settings are stored on your device.

## How We Use Information
Local settings are used only to provide the experience you expect, such as remembering appearance preferences and reminder configuration. We do not sell personal information or use local settings for cross-app advertising profiles.

## Third-Party Services
App stores, operating systems, and crash-reporting services may collect technical data according to their own policies. Those services are controlled by their providers, not by Audio Rescue.

## Data Retention
Locally stored data remains until you clear app data or uninstall the app. Because sessions are not synced to our servers, there is no remote account for us to delete.
